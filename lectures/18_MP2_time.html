Confidence Intervals and Effect Sizes

Confidence Intervals

\[(\bar{y_i}-\bar{y_j}) \pm t^*\cdot SD \sqrt{1/n_i+1/n_j}\]

Effect size

\[D_{ij} = \frac{(\bar{y_i}-\bar{y_j})}{SD}\]

  • Where \(SD = \sqrt{MSE}\)
